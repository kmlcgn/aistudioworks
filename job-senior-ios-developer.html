Senior iOS Developer

Full-Time, Remote

About Parthenius AI Studio

Parthenius AI Studio is a fast-growing startup that develops innovative iOS and web applications powered by the latest AI models. We serve over 100,000 users monthly with products that blend cutting-edge technology and delightful user experiences. As a self-funded company with $1.2M invested into our vision, we have the freedom to pursue big ideas quickly – moving from concept to launch in days. We're building long-term, deeply creative and useful AI-driven experiences for individual creators and businesses alike. If you're looking to join a fast-moving, product-driven team and build apps that users love, you're in the right place!

What You'll Do

Lead iOS Development: Lead development of new iOS features and even entire apps from the ground up, turning ideas into polished products. Own the app architecture and drive performance optimizations to keep our codebases clean, scalable, and lightning-fast.

API & Backend Integration: Integrate third-party APIs and webhooks cleanly and reliably, connecting our apps with external services without missing a beat. Manage Firebase/GCP integrations, maintaining secure and efficient connections to Realtime DB, Firestore, Auth, Cloud Messaging, etc., for a seamless backend/frontend experience.

Collaboration & Code Quality: Collaborate closely with designers, product owners, and other engineers — both asynchronously and in real-time — to iterate on features and ship improvements quickly. Conduct code reviews and champion best practices, continually improving our processes and setting technical direction to raise the bar for the team.

Requirements

3+ years of professional iOS development experience, with a track record of shipping high-quality apps to real users.

Strong command of SwiftUI and core iOS frameworks (Swift, UIKit, etc.), and the ability to build beautiful, responsive UIs.

Hands-on experience with Firebase & GCP – you're familiar with Realtime Database, Firestore, Auth, Cloud Messaging, and can integrate cloud services and external APIs/webhooks without breaking a sweat.

Solid architectural instincts – ability to design maintainable, scalable codebases and make smart trade-offs to keep projects lean and stable as they grow.

Experience delivering user-centric apps – you have built or contributed significantly to iOS apps used by a significant number of users, and you understand the importance of feedback and iteration.

Excellent communication skills in English – especially in writing. You can collaborate asynchronously, articulate technical ideas clearly, and thrive in a remote, written communication-friendly environment.

Self-driven & remote-ready mindset – comfortable working autonomously in a fully remote setting. You manage your time well, take initiative, and stay aligned with product goals without hand-holding.

Why Join Parthenius AI Studio

High ownership & autonomy — No micromanagement here. You'll own your projects end-to-end and have the freedom to build and innovate in the way you know best.

Remote-first culture — Work from anywhere on the planet. We collaborate async and respect work-life balance, so you can design your optimal work environment.

Build products users love — We are product-first (not slide-deck-first). Your work will directly impact users, and you'll get to create apps that real people genuinely enjoy using.

Small, sharp, fast team — Join a tight-knit crew of smart, driven builders who respect your time and talent. We move quickly and treat each other like professionals.

Plenty of room to grow — Technically, creatively, and financially. As Parthenius grows, you'll have opportunities to expand your skill set, take on more responsibility, and reap the rewards of building something great.

Ready to build AI-powered apps that could reach millions? Apply now and help us shape the future of creative AI experiences at Parthenius AI Studio. We can't wait to meet you!

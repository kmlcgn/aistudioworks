Generative AI Teknoloji A.Ş. is an innovative startup building a web application powered by generative AI. This is a hands-on role: you'll design features, integrate APIs, and see your code in production – all while gaining experience in a rapidly growing field.

We value curiosity and a "learn-by-doing" attitude. You'll join a small, agile team where learning new technologies is expected. English is our working language, and communication skills are important.

Responsibilities

  • Build and improve the web UI: Implement and refine pages and components of our web application, focusing on usability, responsiveness, and performance.
  • Work on AI-driven features: Help build flows where users can interact with AI-powered tools (e.g., content generation, personalization, smart templates).

  • Interest in AI: Genuine enthusiasm for AI and modern web products. You don't need to be an ML expert, but you should be excited to work on AI-driven applications.
  • Team player with solid English: Good communication and collaboration skills. All work (code, documentation, discussions) is in English, so strong written and spoken English is required.
  • Self-motivated learner: We're looking for someone curious and proactive. You should be able to pick up new tools quickly, ask questions when needed, and work with minimal supervision.

  • Salary: 40,000 TL net per month (Turkish Lira), paid monthly.
  • Tools & Support: You'll have access to the necessary tools and APIs, plus guidance from more experienced developers. We encourage mentorship, code reviews, and pair programming as part of your learning.

Why Join Us?

Part-Time Remote

Performance Marketing Specialist

Remote 15–20 hours/week Contract

About the Role

Generative AI Teknoloji A.Ş. is a fast-growing mobile AI company building consumer-facing iOS and Web apps. We're looking for an experienced Performance Marketing Specialist who can own and scale our Google Ads (Search only) and Meta Ads for iOS user acquisition and create new campaigns.

This is not a junior or training role. You should already know how to acquire high-quality iOS users under modern privacy constraints (ATT, SKAN, AEM, etc.) and be able to work independently with clear performance goals.

You will work part-time (15–20 hours per week) on a contract basis, with flexible hours as long as you can join for a few overlapping hours with our core team for syncs and reporting.

Responsibilities

Google Ads – Search Only

  • Plan, launch, and optimize Google Search campaigns to drive high-intent users to our iOS app (App Store page or landing pages).
  • Build and maintain keyword structures, including match types and negative keyword lists.
  • Own bidding logic and strategies (e.g., tCPA / tROAS style thinking applied to Search campaigns).
  • Collaborate with the tech team to ensure proper conversion tracking via Firebase/GA4 and/or server-side tracking in a SKAN-compliant way.
  • Run ongoing A/B tests on ad copy and extensions to improve CTR, CVR, and downstream ROAS.

Meta Ads – iOS User Acquisition

  • Plan and execute Meta campaigns (Facebook & Instagram) focused on iOS app installs, trials, subscriptions, and in-app purchases.
  • Set up and maintain Aggregated Event Measurement (AEM) and event prioritization.
  • Work with the product/creative team to brief and iterate on UGC creatives, hooks, and ad angles.
  • Structure campaigns logically (prospecting, retargeting, broad vs. interest, lookalikes, Advantage+ app style setups where relevant).
  • Continuously optimize budgets, bids, and audiences based on CPI, CPA, ROAS, LTV, and retention.

Attribution, Measurement & Strategy

  • Collaborate with developers on SKAdNetwork (SKAN) setup and conversion value schema (early signals vs. revenue signals, etc.).
  • Use data from MMPs (AppsFlyer, Adjust, Branch, Singular, etc.), Firebase/GA4, or similar tools to evaluate cohort performance.
  • Prepare weekly performance reports (installs, trials, subscriptions, revenue, ROAS by campaign/ad set/ad) and propose next steps.
  • Suggest and prioritize creative tests, GEO/language expansion, and budget reallocations based on data.

What We're Looking For

Must-Haves

  • 2+ years of hands-on experience managing Google Ads Search and Meta Ads specifically for mobile apps (iOS).
  • Solid practical experience with:
    • SKAdNetwork (SKAN) (conversion schemas, postbacks, limitations)
    • ATT (App Tracking Transparency) and its impact on tracking & optimization
    • AEM (Aggregated Event Measurement) on Meta
    • At least one MMP (AppsFlyer, Adjust, Branch, Singular, etc.)
  • Strong understanding of app growth metrics: CPI, CPA, CAC, ROAS, ARPU, LTV, retention, payback period, cohort analysis
  • Ability to design, launch, and optimize campaign structures from scratch for both Google Search and Meta.
  • Comfortable working independently with clear performance targets and minimal micromanagement.
  • Strong written and spoken English for daily communication with a global team.

Nice-to-Haves

  • Background with subscription-based consumer apps.
  • Familiarity with Firebase, GA4, or similar BI/reporting tools.
  • Experience testing and scaling UGC-style creatives on Reels, TikTok, Shorts, etc.
  • Any prior work with AI / creative tools is a plus.

Working Conditions & Compensation

  • Schedule: Part-time, ~20 hours per week, flexible.
  • Location: Fully remote.
  • Contract Type: Freelance / contract (initial 3 months, extendable based on results).
  • Compensation: 40,000–60,000 TRY net per month (or 800–1,200 USD) plus bonuses.
  • High performers can expect review and upside potential as we scale.

Why Join Us?

  • Own the user acquisition strategy for a growing iOS and Web product in the AI space.
  • Work directly with founders and product owners on high-impact growth decisions.
  • Plenty of room to experiment, test, and learn – we are data-driven, not bureaucracy-driven.
  • Flexible, remote-friendly setup with autonomy and clear performance expectations.

How to Apply

Send an email to hello@aistudio.works with the subject line: "Part-Time Performance Marketing Specialist"

Include:

  • Your CV or LinkedIn profile
  • A short intro paragraph about your experience with iOS UA, SKAN, AEM, and MMPs
  • 1–2 short case studies or examples where you:
    • Scaled an iOS app on Meta and/or Google Search
    • Improved ROAS or reduced CPI/CPA through specific optimizations

If you live and breathe iOS performance marketing and want a flexible, impactful part-time role, we'd love to hear from you.

Email Marketing Specialist

Remote Full-Time Contract

About the Role

Generative AI Teknoloji A.Ş. is seeking an experienced Email Marketing Specialist to drive our email user acquisition and engagement. In this fully remote role, you will focus exclusively on email marketing for our consumer-facing iOS and web apps, with the goal of converting new users (cold and lightly warm leads) into active customers.

You will own the entire email campaign process end-to-end: building and segmenting targeted mailing lists, writing compelling copy, designing email layouts, executing A/B tests, and analyzing performance.

Responsibilities

  • Build and segment mailing lists: Identify, grow, and manage targeted email lists to reach new potential users and maintain list quality and hygiene.
  • Craft end-to-end campaigns: Design and execute email campaigns from start to finish – write persuasive, on-brand copy; design or coordinate email templates (mobile-responsive); set up automations and schedule sends using email marketing tools.
  • A/B testing & optimization: Implement A/B tests on subject lines, content, and layouts; track results and continuously optimize for higher open rates, CTRs, and conversions.
  • Analytics & reporting: Monitor and analyze key email metrics (open rate, click-through rate, conversion rate, bounce rate, unsubscribe rate, etc.) and use insights to refine targeting, content, and sequencing.
  • Deliverability & compliance: Ensure high deliverability by following best practices in list management, sending reputation, and compliance with relevant regulations (opt-in, CAN-SPAM, GDPR, etc.).
  • Collaboration: Work with designers, product managers, and the broader marketing team to align emails with brand voice, product launches, campaigns, and seasonal promotions.
  • Ownership & strategy: Take full ownership of the email marketing pipeline – from strategy and planning, to execution and reporting – operating independently in a remote environment.

Required Experience and Qualifications

  • Email marketing expertise: Proven experience as an Email Marketing Specialist or similar role (typically 3+ years), preferably in consumer apps or SaaS businesses.
  • Platform proficiency: Hands-on experience with modern email/automation tools such as Mailchimp, Customer.io, Klaviyo, Braze, or similar platforms (list management, segmentation, automation, analytics).
  • Copywriting & creativity: Excellent email copywriting skills and a strong sense of visual hierarchy; ability to create engaging emails and templates (or confidently brief and direct designers).
  • Analytical skills: Strong analytical mindset and experience with A/B testing and performance optimization. Comfortable interpreting email metrics and translating them into concrete actions.
  • Tech-savvy targeting: Skilled in audience segmentation, personalization, and sequencing, especially for cold and lightly warm leads, re-engagement flows, and lifecycle campaigns.
  • Communication: Excellent written and verbal English; able to clearly communicate campaign strategies, test plans, and results to the team.
  • Self-motivation: Highly organized, proactive, and able to work independently, managing multiple campaigns from concept through to conversion and reporting.

Nice-to-Haves

  • Experience marketing iOS/mobile apps or SaaS products
  • Familiarity with product and marketing analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)

Working Conditions & Compensation

  • Schedule: Full-time, flexible hours. (Some overlap with Europe/Turkey working hours is preferred.)
  • Location: 100% remote – work from anywhere with a stable internet connection.
  • Contract Type: Freelance / contract-based, long-term collaboration preferred.
  • Salary: Competitive, in the range of 40,000–60,000 TRY net per month (approximately $1,300–$2,000 USD equivalent, depending on experience and agreement).

Why Join Us?

If you meet these qualifications and are excited to fully own and scale our email marketing channel, we'd love to hear from you.

Include:

  • Your CV and a short introduction (in English) outlining your relevant email marketing experience
  • If possible, include 1–2 brief case studies (or concrete examples) of campaigns you've run, including goals, your approach, and the results

Product & Market Research

About the Role

Generative AI Teknoloji A.Ş. is building AI-powered web and mobile products for creators and everyday users. We're looking for a working student who is excited about product, AI, and UX to support us with market research, competitor analysis, and new feature ideas.

This role is like a junior product owner / product researcher: you'll explore what competitors are doing, what users might want, and help us shape the next features of our apps. You do not need prior job experience – interest, curiosity, and willingness to learn are more important.

You'll work part-time alongside your studies (around 15–20 hours per week), fully remote, on a freelance contract.

What You'll Do

Research the Market & Competitors

  • Map out competing apps and tools in the AI / creative / productivity space
  • Document their features, pricing, positioning, and strengths/weaknesses
  • Create simple comparison overviews (tables, slides, Notion pages)

Generate and Structure New Feature Ideas

  • Brainstorm ideas for new features and improvements to our web & iOS apps
  • Turn raw ideas into structured concepts (problem, solution, target user, value)
  • Prepare short concept docs that the team can quickly understand and discuss

Help "Architect" Features

  • Support writing basic user stories and user flows (e.g., "As a user I want to…")
  • Sketch simple screens/flows (even on paper or with simple tools)
  • Think through edge cases and user experience details together with the team

Collect and Organize Insights

  • Gather user feedback from reviews, support conversations, and surveys
  • Tag and summarize recurring themes, pains, and feature requests
  • Maintain a simple "idea backlog" and help keep it tidy and prioritized

Support Roadmap & Prioritization

  • Help prepare lightweight prioritization (impact vs. effort, etc.)
  • Assist in preparing internal summaries or mini-presentations for upcoming features

Learn How Product Is Built

  • Join product discussions and planning calls
  • Observe how design and development teams turn specs into real features

What We're Looking For

  • Student status: Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's program (e.g., Business, Economics, Computer Science, Design, Data Science, Engineering, or similar). This is a working-student style, part-time role.
  • Strong interest in product & tech: Curious about how digital products are built and improved. Excited by AI, mobile apps, SaaS, and/or creator tools.
  • Analytical & organized: Enjoys researching, comparing, and structuring information. Able to create clear summaries instead of long, messy notes.
  • Good communication in English: Comfortable reading/writing in English (our working language). Can express ideas clearly in writing and in calls.
  • Proactive learner: No professional experience required – but you should be eager to learn. Comfortable asking questions, proposing ideas, and taking ownership of small tasks.

Nice-to-Haves (Not Required)

  • Any experience with Notion, Figma, Miro, or similar tools
  • Side projects, hackathons, case competitions, or relevant coursework

Working Conditions & Compensation

  • Hours: Part-time, approx. 15–20 hours per week. Flexible schedule (e.g., 3–4 half days per week) that can be adapted to your classes and exams.
  • Location: 100% remote – you can work from anywhere with a stable internet connection.
  • Contract Type: Freelance / contract-based, working-student style (initial 3–6 months, with the option to extend).
  • Salary: 40,000 TL net per month (Turkish Lira), paid monthly.

Why This Role Is Interesting

  • You'll get real product experience without needing prior jobs or internships.
  • You'll see how ideas move from market research → concept → spec → real feature.
  • You'll work closely with the founders and product team, not buried in busywork.
  • You'll learn how a modern AI product is built and grow into a more senior product role over time if it's a good fit.

How to Apply

We're excited to see how you think and how you can help us shape the next features of our AI products!

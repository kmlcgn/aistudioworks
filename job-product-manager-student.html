Part-Time Remote Student

Working Student Product Manager

About Parthenius AI Studio

Parthenius AI Studio is a fast-growing startup that develops innovative iOS and web applications powered by the latest AI models. We serve over 100,000 users monthly with products that blend cutting-edge technology and delightful user experiences. As a self-funded company with $1.2M invested into our vision, we have the freedom to pursue big ideas quickly – moving from concept to launch in days. We're building long-term, deeply creative and useful AI-driven experiences for individual creators and businesses alike. If you're looking to join a fast-moving, product-driven team and build apps that users love, you're in the right place!

What You'll Do

User Research & Product Discovery: Engage with users through surveys, interviews, and testing to understand their needs and pain points. Gather insights and translate feedback into actionable product improvements.

Market & Competitor Analysis: Research industry trends and analyze competitor products to identify opportunities for our AI-powered apps to stand out and better serve users.

Feature Ideation & Scoping: Brainstorm new features and enhancements with the team. Write product specs and user stories to clearly communicate what to build and why, ensuring design and engineering teams have clear direction.

Project Coordination: Work closely with designers and developers to bring features from concept to launch. Coordinate timelines, clarify requirements, and ensure the final product meets user needs and vision.

Product Documentation & Iteration: Maintain product documentation and roadmaps (Notion, PRDs, release checklists). Monitor feature performance post-launch, gather user feedback, and iterate to continuously improve the user experience.

Requirements

Student Status: Enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's program. This role is for a working student (~15–20 hours/week alongside your studies). Your field of study is less important than your skills and passion, though relevant fields like computer science, business, design, etc. are a plus.

Product Experience: Some exposure to product management or product development – through an internship, side project, or relevant coursework. You understand how software products are built and have contributed in some way before.

User-Centric Mindset: Passion for understanding user problems and curiosity about what makes products work. You enjoy gathering feedback and researching what users truly need.

Analytical Skills: Comfortable with research and basic data analysis. You can draw insights from user feedback or usage metrics and base decisions on evidence.

Strong Communication: Clear expression in writing and conversation. Whether drafting a feature spec, talking with users, or explaining requirements to engineers, you know how to get your point across.

Organized & Proactive: Good time management to balance school and work. You take ownership of tasks, meet deadlines, and proactively address problems or opportunities you identify.

Why Join Parthenius AI Studio

Real Product Ownership: This is a real product role – not a coffee-fetching internship. From day one, you'll own features and product areas that matter. Your work will be in the hands of actual users, and you'll see the impact of your decisions in real time.

Real Ownership, No Micromanagement: We don't micromanage. You'll have autonomy to try out your ideas and run projects your way. We encourage experimentation – if something doesn't work, learn from it and move on to the next idea.

Fast Learning & Growth: Join a small, agile team where you'll wear many hats. You'll learn faster here than anywhere else – working closely with experienced founders and the product team on challenging problems. As the company grows, so can your role and responsibilities.

Flexible Remote Work: Work when and where you perform best. This position is remote with flexible scheduling – you choose the days and hours (around 15–20 per week) that fit your classes and life.

Competitive Compensation: We offer a highly competitive salary for a part-time student role. We believe great talent deserves great pay, and we'll make sure you feel valued for your contributions.

Startup Culture & Fun: Experience a true startup environment with a friendly, casual vibe. We celebrate innovation, encourage trying new things (even if they fail), and enjoy the journey. No bureaucracy – just a focus on building awesome things together.

Ready to build AI-powered apps that could reach millions? Apply now and help us shape the future of creative AI experiences at Parthenius AI Studio. We can't wait to meet you!

How to Apply

Send an email to hello@aistudio.works with the subject line: "Working Student – Product Manager"

Please include:

Your CV and a short introduction (in your preferred language, even 2–3 sentences is enough) explaining why you're interested in this role.

Links to any relevant projects, portfolios, or past work (if available). Examples could include: a product spec or user story you wrote, a design or wireframe (Figma links welcome!), a Notion page from a project you managed, or a class project involving product research.

Your expected salary for this position.

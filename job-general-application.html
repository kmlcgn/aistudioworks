General Application – Join Parthenius AI Studio

About Parthenius AI Studio

Parthenius AI Studio is a fast-growing mobile AI company building consumer-facing iOS and web applications. We are a small, bootstrapped team driven by curiosity and innovation, already serving over 100,000 users each month with our cutting-edge AI products. And we're just getting started.

This isn't a listing for one specific role – it's an open invitation. If you're bright, curious, passionate, and determined to push the boundaries of technology, we want to hear from you. We build meaningful tools by taking the latest in generative AI and turning it into intuitive, useful products that people love. Sound exciting? Read on!

Who We Are

At Parthenius, we pride ourselves on transforming advanced AI research into real-world solutions. We're working on latest Generative AI models and making their capabilities accessible through friendly, easy-to-use interfaces. In other words, we love building tools that empower creators and users alike.

As a bootstrapped startup, we move fast and iterate constantly. Every team member wears multiple hats and directly impacts the product. Experience is valued, but potential and attitude matter even more – we believe the right environment can turn raw potential into a world-class skill set, and even those with little or no experience can enjoy unusually fast growth here.

Who We're Looking For

We're more interested in you than in any one predefined role. Whether your expertise is in coding, design, product, AI research, or something totally unique, if you have skills and ideas that could amplify our mission, we'd love to hear about it. Think of this as an opportunity to craft your own role. You might be a great fit if you identify with the following:

Problem Solver & Learner: You dive into new challenges head-first and figure things out as you go. Curiosity isn't just a trait – it's how you live your life. You love learning by doing and aren't afraid to venture beyond your comfort zone.

Creative & Independent: You think outside the box and aren't afraid to chart your own path. Like the best startup talent, you're dynamic, creative, and bring an independent way of thinking. You can self-manage when needed, but you also know how to collaborate when it counts.

Passionate & Impact-Driven: You're not just looking for a job; you want to build something that matters. You have the drive to elevate those around you (and be elevated by them), and you thrive on projects that can make a real-world impact. "Good enough" isn't in your vocabulary – you aim high and hunger for achievement.

Ownership Mindset: You take responsibility from day one and treat projects as your own. We love people who are ready to "own their thing" – you won't be a newcomer here; you'll have the autonomy to run with your ideas. Accountability, initiative, and pride in your work are second nature to you.

Why You'll Love It Here

Remote-First & Flexible: Work from anywhere (yes, anywhere with a reliable internet connection) on a schedule that fits your life. We care about results, not clocking hours. There are none of the bad bits like micromanaging or rigid 9-to-5 hours here – choose when and where you work best.

Real Ownership & Autonomy: Have an idea? Run with it. See an issue? You can fix it. We give you real responsibility and trust you to lead initiatives. No one will be breathing down your neck; no micromanagement means you're free to choose a direction, test, fail, and try the next idea. You'll have the latitude to make decisions and drive projects, even as a part-timer.

Impact & Fast Growth: Your work will directly shape products used by thousands today (and potentially millions tomorrow). We foster a culture that optimizes for massive impact – it's OK to miss, but never to aim small. Bold ideas are encouraged. In our tight-knit team, you'll take on significant responsibilities and grow your skills unusually quickly. Every challenge is a chance to elevate your craft.

Collaborative, High-Energy Culture: Join an intensely curious, vibrant team that thrives on collaboration and continuous learning. Egos are left at the door; we champion selfless teamwork and like-minded passion. Everyone's ideas get a fair hearing, whether you've been here a day or a year. We value curiosity and a "learn-by-doing" attitude in everything we do – you'll find support to experiment, learn, and level up every step of the way.

Ready to build AI-powered apps that could reach millions? Apply now and help us shape the future of creative AI experiences at Parthenius AI Studio. We can't wait to meet you!

How to Apply

Excited? We'd love to hear from you. To apply, please send an email to hello@aistudio.works with the subject line: "General Application – [Your Name]"

Include:

CV/Resume – highlight your background and any experience you're proud of.

Brief Introduction – a few lines about yourself (in your preferred language) and what excites you about joining Parthenius. Let us know how you think you can contribute.

Relevant Links – links to any project repositories, portfolios, or past work you'd like to share (GitHub, personal website, articles, etc.).

Expected Salary – your compensation expectations for a part-time contract role (monthly, Turkish Lira).

We review every general application carefully. If you're passionate about building the next generation of AI-powered tools and don't want to be boxed into a conventional job posting, we want to hear from you. Take the leap and reach out – you just might be the talent we didn't know we needed. We can't wait to see what you'll bring to the team!

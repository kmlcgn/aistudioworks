Part-Time Remote Student

Working Student – Software Engineer

About Parthenius AI Studio

Parthenius AI Studio is a fast-growing startup that develops innovative iOS and web applications powered by the latest AI models. We serve over 100,000 users monthly with products that blend cutting-edge technology and delightful user experiences. As a self-funded company with $1.2M invested into our vision, we have the freedom to pursue big ideas quickly – moving from concept to launch in days. We're building long-term, deeply creative and useful AI-driven experiences for individual creators and businesses alike. If you're looking to join a fast-moving, product-driven team and build apps that users love, you're in the right place!

What You'll Do

Full-Stack Development: Maintain and enhance our web app's frontend (Next.js/React) and backend (Firebase) to deliver new features and improvements.

AI Integration: Research, experiment with, and compare state-of-the-art AI models to meet our needs. Integrate the best AI solutions into our app to make it smarter and more useful for users.

Optimize and Refactor: Continuously improve the app's performance, stability, and code quality through thorough testing, profiling, and peer code reviews. Tackle bugs or bottlenecks and ensure a smooth user experience.

Requirements

Experience with React & Next.js: Proficiency in modern front-end development using React/Next.js and TypeScript to build rich user interfaces.

Firebase & Backend Know-How: Strong experience with Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Cloud Functions, etc.) and general web backend development.

State Management: Ability to manage complex client-side state in a large application.

Security Awareness: Familiarity with web app security best practices (protecting user data, handling keys/credentials, writing secure database rules, etc.).

API Integrations: Experience integrating third-party APIs and services (e.g. for payments, analytics, or other SaaS tools) into applications.

Student Status: Enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's program in Computer Science (or related field). This role is for a working student (~20 hours/week alongside your studies).

Why Join Parthenius AI Studio

Build Products People Love: We love crafting apps that people find genuinely helpful and enjoyable. Your work will directly impact thousands of users, and you'll get feedback fast.

Real Ownership, No Micromanagement: From day one, you'll own your projects and contribute ideas. We don't believe in micromanagement or rigid hours – we trust you to deliver results in your own style.

Fast Learning & Growth: Join a small, agile team where you'll wear many hats. You'll learn faster here than anywhere else – working closely with experienced founders and engineers on challenging problems. As the company grows, so can your role and responsibilities.

Flexible Remote Work: Work when and where you perform best. This position is remote with flexible scheduling – you choose the days and hours (around 20 per week) that fit your classes and life.

Competitive Compensation: We offer a highly competitive salary for a part-time student role. We believe great talent deserves great pay, and we'll make sure you feel valued for your contributions.

Startup Culture & Fun: Experience a true startup environment with a friendly, casual vibe. We celebrate innovation, encourage trying new things (even if they fail), and enjoy the journey. No bureaucracy – just a focus on building awesome things together.

Ready to build AI-powered apps that could reach millions? Apply now and help us shape the future of creative AI experiences at Parthenius AI Studio. We can't wait to meet you!

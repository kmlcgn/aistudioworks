Working Student – Email Marketing Specialist

Part-Time, Remote

About Parthenius AI Studio

Parthenius AI Studio is a fast-growing startup that develops innovative iOS and web applications powered by the latest AI models. We serve over 100,000 users monthly with products that blend cutting-edge technology and delightful user experiences. As a self-funded company with $1.2M invested into our vision, we have the freedom to pursue big ideas quickly – moving from concept to launch in days. We're building long-term, deeply creative and useful AI-driven experiences for individual creators and businesses alike. If you're looking to join a fast-moving, product-driven team and build apps that users love, you're in the right place!

What You'll Do

Plan & Execute Email Campaigns: Assist in designing, scheduling, and sending out email marketing campaigns and newsletters. Work on creating engaging content (text and basic HTML/CSS) for different audiences.

Segmentation & Personalization: Help segment our contact lists and personalize email content for various customer groups to improve engagement. Ensure each subscriber receives relevant, targeted content.

Automation & Sequences: Learn to use our email marketing platform to set up multi-step email sequences and automated workflows (e.g. welcome series, follow-ups). You'll have the chance to automate tasks that streamline our outreach.

Deliverability Management: Work with the team to ensure our emails land in inboxes (not spam). This includes monitoring email deliverability metrics like bounce rates and open rates, and learning about email authentication best practices (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) to maintain a good sender reputation.

Analytics & Optimization: Track campaign performance (open rates, click-through rates, conversions) and analyze results. Create simple reports or dashboards to visualize key metrics, and suggest improvements for future campaigns based on data.

Continuous Improvement: Stay curious and proactive. Research current email marketing trends and best practices, and bring new ideas to the table. Test different subject lines, content, or send times to optimize results.

Collaborative Projects: Work closely with our marketing and tech team to implement campaign ideas. Coordinate with teammates to gather content, ensure emails are consistent with our brand, and troubleshoot any technical issues (with plenty of support and guidance).

Requirements

Student Status: Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's program in Industrial Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Business Informatics, or a related field.

Eager to Learn: No prior work experience in email marketing required! What's most important is your willingness to learn and enthusiasm for digital marketing. If you're curious about how email systems work and how to make marketing decisions based on data, you'll fit right in.

Technical Aptitude: Comfortable with technology and quick to pick up new tools. You don't need to be a software developer, but basic understanding of HTML/CSS or any programming/scripting experience is a plus (you'll use it to tweak email templates or work with APIs/automation tools).

Analytical Mindset: Enjoy working with numbers and data. You can pay attention to details (like spotting a typo or noticing a drop in open rate) and are eager to learn how to interpret metrics to make things better.

Problem-Solving Attitude: When faced with a challenge (say, an email that isn't delivering or a list that needs cleaning), you approach it logically and aren't afraid to ask questions or research solutions.

Communication Skills: Good written and verbal communication in English. You can explain your ideas or report findings clearly. (Bonus if you're comfortable writing content in English for our emails.)

Team Player: Able to work collaboratively in a remote team environment. You're proactive about updating others on your progress and willing to assist teammates. Being remote, you should also be self-motivated and responsible with managing your own time.

Why Join Parthenius AI Studio

Real-World Experience: Get hands-on experience with cutting-edge email marketing tools and practices. You'll work on actual campaigns that reach our audience, giving you valuable experience that goes beyond the classroom.

Mentorship & Training: We don't expect you to know it all on day one. You'll receive guidance from experienced team members and have access to resources to learn about email campaign management, automation, and deliverability. This is a growth opportunity – as you show initiative, we'll support you in picking up new skills (from using APIs to understanding marketing strategy).

Flexible Schedule: As a working student, we understand your studies come first. We offer flexible working hours (around 20 hours per week, with the ability to adjust around exams or classes). Work remotely from wherever you are comfortable, as long as you can collaborate during agreed-upon times.

Innovative Environment: Join a small, agile team where your contributions truly matter. Your ideas will be heard, and you'll have the creative freedom to experiment with new approaches in a supportive setting.

Career Growth: Successful collaboration could open the door to future opportunities with us (extended contracts or even a full-time role after graduation). At the very least, you'll build a great resume story and reference by taking ownership of meaningful projects.

Ready to build AI-powered apps that could reach millions? Apply now and help us shape the future of creative AI experiences at Parthenius AI Studio. We can't wait to meet you!
